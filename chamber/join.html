Applicant Information
Membership Level
Gold Membership

Gold Membership:

  • Exclusive access to high-profile networking events with industry leaders.
  • Priority placement in chamber publications and directories.
  • Complimentary tickets to premium workshops and seminars.
  • Personalized introductions to potential business partners and clients.
  • Dedicated account manager for personalized support and assistance.
  • Logo placement on the chamber's website and promotional materials.
  • Opportunity to host or sponsor chamber events for increased visibility.

Silver Membership:

  • Access to chamber networking events and mixers.
  • Listing in chamber publications and directories.
  • Discounted rates for workshops, seminars, and advertising opportunities.
  • Invitation to member-only educational sessions and panels.
  • Access to chamber resources, such as market research reports and business tools.
  • Recognition as a Silver Member on the chamber's website.
  • Referral services connecting businesses with potential clients or partners.

Bronze Membership:

  • Entry-level access to chamber networking events.
  • Basic listing in chamber publications and directories.
  • Discounted rates for select workshops, seminars, and advertising.
  • Access to chamber resources, including online forums and webinars.
  • Opportunity to participate in chamber committees and task forces.
  • Bronze Member badge for display on website and marketing materials.
  • Member-to-member discounts with other chamber businesses.

Non-profit Organizations
Free Membership:

  • Access to select chamber networking events and mixers.
  • Basic online listing in chamber directories.
  • Limited access to chamber resources, such as online newsletters.
  • Invitation to select educational sessions and workshops.
  • Opportunity to attend chamber-sponsored community events.
  • Free Member badge for display on website and marketing materials.
  • Access to chamber job postings and internship opportunities.

Site Name

Valley Stream Chamber of Commerce

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to promote the business interests of the members of the organization and promote the community, attracting additional business, tourism, and people.

The mission of the Valley Stream Chamber of Commerce is to promote business, add value to our community, foster a beneficial relationship with the community, and help improve the overall quality of life in the Village of Valley Stream and surrounding communities.
